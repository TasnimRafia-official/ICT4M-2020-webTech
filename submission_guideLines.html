CONTACT FEES & REGISTRATION

All paper submissions will be handled electronically. Papers must be submitted electronically in DOC format Submiited papers must not substantially overlap with papers that have been published or that are simultaneously submitted to a journal or a conference with proceedings. Submitted papers will be peerreviewed and carefully evaluated based on originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of exposition.

The submitted papers must be between 4-6 pages. To facilitate the paper's double blind peerreview process, authors should make efforts to ensure that information about the authors' identities do not appear anywhere in the text body of the paper and must follow the guideleines.

Upon acceptance, at least one of the authors listed in the paper must submit the final paper, attend the conference and present the paper according to the schedule of the conference. In the event the presenter is unable to attend the conference for any reason, your accepted paper will not be considered for submission of the proceeding to the IEEE Xplore.

NO SHOW
POLICY

Papers that are accepted must be presented at the conference, either by the authors themselved or via a proxy.

In case a paper is not presented at the conference, it shall be deemed a 'no-show'. Only presented papers will be submitted for for indexing. No refund of the paid fees may be claimed by the 'no-show' author.
