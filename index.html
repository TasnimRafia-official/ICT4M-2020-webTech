Overview

ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians,
researchers, practitioners, professionals, and students to
address the challenges of Information and
Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world.
This conference has been conducted seven times
so far with the most recent location
in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 23-25 July 2018.

THEME FOR THIS YEAR

Mental Health and Well Being in the 4th Industrial Revolution

In conjunction with the World Congress on Integration and Islamicization, ICT4M 2020 will be held on 27-29 March 2020 in Kuantan, Pahang.

All accepted papers will be submitted for indexing in IEEE Xplore. Selected papers will be invited for publications in Scopus journals
